Property Management

NRI Service Desk, we specialize in comprehensive property management solutions tailored for Non-Resident Indians (NRIs). Our goal is to ensure that your property in India remains well-maintained, legally secure, and generates optimal returns, even while you reside abroad. With a dedicated team of professionals, we handle everything from property maintenance and tenant management to legal and financial assistance.

  • Property Maintenance & Upkeep
  • Tenant Management
  • Legal & Financial Assistance
  • Buy & Sell Assistance
  • End-to-End Property Monitoring

Nurse Services

Owning property in India while residing abroad can be challenging, especially when it comes to ensuring the well-being of elderly family members or individuals requiring special care. Our dedicated Nurse Services are designed to provide compassionate and professional assistance to NRIs and their families.

  • Elderly Care & Home Healthcare
  • Medical Coordination & Emergency Support
  • Rehabilitation & Post-Hospitalization Care
  • Home Nursing & Specialized Care

Old Age Home

When living abroad, NRIs often seek safe, comfortable, and compassionate living arrangements for their aging parents. Our Old Age Home services provide a nurturing environment with round-the-clock care, ensuring the well-being of elderly residents.

  • Comfortable Living Facilities
  • 24/7 Medical & Personal Care
  • Social Engagement & Wellness
  • Nutritious Meals & Dietary Support
  • Personalized care plans based on individual needs

House Keeping Services

Maintaining a clean and welcoming home or property in India can be challenging for NRIs living abroad. Our professional housekeeping services ensure that your property remains spotless, well-maintained, and ready for your visits or tenants.

  • Regular cleaning and maintenance
  • Deep cleaning for residential and commercial properties
  • Pest control and sanitization
  • Waste disposal and recycling management
  • Laundry and home organization services

Goverment Documents

Managing government documents from abroad can be a complex and daunting task for NRIs. Our specialized Government Document Assistance services ensure that all your essential paperwork is handled with efficiency, accuracy, and compliance with Indian regulations.

  • Document Procurement & Renewal
  • Property-Related Documentation
  • Legal & Compliance Support
  • Taxation & Financial Documentation
  • NRI bank account documentation and compliance

Interior/Exterior Works
