About Us

Our Service, Your Home

At NRI Service Desk, we are your dedicated help desk, committed to simplifying life for Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) worldwide. From legal and financial matters to property management, elder care, home nursing, and documentation, we provide expert solutions tailored to your needs.

Our Team

Our team of experienced professionals is committed to delivering fast, reliable, and secure services, so you can have peace of mind while managing your affairs in India. We understand the complexities that NRIs face and are here to provide the support you need to simplify the process.

How It Works

Getting started is simple! Just contact us via our website, phone, or email with your service request. Our experts will assess your needs and provide the best support in India for NRIs. We handle everything professionally, keep you updated, and ensure a smooth, hassle-free experience.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and go-to service provider for NRIs, ensuring seamless support across borders.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between NRIs and their needs in India with reliable, hassle-free, and professional assistance—whether it’s managing your assets, caring for your elders, or securing essential services.
