Property Management

NRI Service Desk, we specialize in comprehensive property management solutions tailored for Non-Resident Indians (NRIs). Our goal is to ensure that your property in Tamil Nadu India remains well-maintained, legally secure, and generates optimal returns, even while you reside abroad. With a dedicated team of professionals, we handle everything from property maintenance and tenant management to legal and financial assistance.

  • Property Maintenance & Upkeep
  • Tenant Management
  • Legal & Financial Assistance
  • Buy & Sell Assistance
  • End-to-End Property Monitoring

Nurse Services

Owning property in Tamil Nadu, India while residing abroad can be challenging, especially when it comes to ensuring the well-being of elderly family members or individuals requiring special care. Our dedicated Nurse Services are designed to provide compassionate and professional assistance to NRIs and their families.

  • Elderly Care & Home Healthcare
  • Medical Coordination & Emergency Support
  • Rehabilitation & Post-Hospitalization Care
  • Home Nursing & Specialized Care

Eldery Care

When living abroad, NRIs often seek safe, comfortable, and compassionate living arrangements for their aging parents in Tamil Nadu, India. Our elderly care services provide a nurturing environment with care, ensuring the well-being of elderly residents.

  • Comfortable Living Facilities
  • 24/7 Medical & Personal Care
  • Social Engagement & Wellness
  • Nutritious Meals & Dietary Support
  • Personalized care plans based on individual needs

House Keeping Services

Maintaining a clean and welcoming home or property in Tamil Nadu, India can be challenging for NRIs living abroad. Our professional housekeeping services ensure that your property remains spotless, well-maintained, and ready for your visits or tenants.

  • Regular cleaning and maintenance
  • Deep cleaning for residential and commercial properties
  • Pest control and sanitization
  • Waste disposal and recycling.
  • Laundry and home organization services

Goverment Documents

Managing government documents from abroad can be a complex and daunting task for NRIs. Our specialized Government Document Assistance services ensure that all your essential paperwork within Tamil Nadu, India is handled with efficiency, accuracy, and compliance with Indian regulations.

  • Document Procurement & Renewal
  • Property-Related Documentation
  • Legal & Compliance Support
  • Taxation & Financial Documentation
  • NRI bank account documentation and compliance

Visa & Immigration Services

As an NRI, navigating visa and immigration processes can be complex and time-consuming. Our Visa and Immigration Services are designed to provide expert guidance, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience for you and your family.

  • OCI & PIO Card Assistance
  • Legal Documentation & Compliance
  • Travel & Relocation Support
